About this project

Hi, my name is Sebastian and I created this project together with some friends to help some other friends manage their indie theater.

They needed a simple tool to take online reservations for their events. It worked out so well that others wanted to join in so we expanded the features list here we are with a total redesign.

For any questions or technical support you can reach us by Facebook or Twitter.
