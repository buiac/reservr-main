Features

  • Online Reservations

    Users can reserv seats for your awesome event.

  • Waiting List
    In case the event runs out of seats, users can still sign-up on the Waiting List. The system automatically asigns free seats to users on the Waiting List.
  • Email Reminders

    Email reminders are sent to users one day before the event. Reminders can be set on or off individually for each event.

  • Notifications

    Get notifications every time someone sign's up for your event. It can be switched on or off as well.

  • Personalize your page

    You can set your own logo on the events page. Check this one out for REACTOR: reservr.net/u/reactor/event/jrCH7NZoW9yRGW5H

  • Templates

    All the notifications messages can be changed according to your language and specific needs.

  • Mailchimp integration (available soon)

    Users who wish to sign-up for your mailing list are automatically sent to your MailChimp list.
